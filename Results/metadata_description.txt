_Description of Columns in metadata.csv:

- L1: Sensor parameter for the first layer.
- L2: Sensor parameter for the second layer.
- L3: Sensor parameter for the third layer.
- accuracy: Accuracy level of the sensors.
- nr_workers: The number of workers involved in the simulation.
- nr_visitors: The number of visitors involved in the simulation.
- Total Iterations: The total number of simulation iterations.
- Number of Injury Cases: The total number of injury cases ('I') during the simulation.
- Risk of Injury: The risk of injury calculated as (number of injuries / total iterations).
- Average Productivity: Average productivity, defined as the mean of 1/Duration for each simulation iteration.
